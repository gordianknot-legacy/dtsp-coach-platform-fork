**Flow** **A:** **Coach** **makes** **a** **call**

**What** **This** **Flow** **Is**

A coaching call is the primary recurring intervention in DTSP. Each
teacher receives 2 coaching calls per month, every month across the
program year — making this the highest-frequency touchpoint and the
operational backbone of the entire program.

The coach is the primary actor. The flow covers the full call lifecycle:
confirmation → pre-call preparation → live call → no-show handling →
reschedule handling →

tech-issue handling → post-call capture → WhatsApp summary → session
close.

Three things define the quality bar for this flow:

> ● The call must be anchored to 1–2 of the four coaching focus areas:
> planning with TG, delivering lessons, assessing student learning, or
> remediating based on evidence — not open-ended conversation
>
> ● Every completed call must produce three outputs before the session
> is closed: a summary of what was discussed and decided, 1–3 specific
> observable action steps, and a confirmed next call date
>
> ● The post-call WhatsApp summary is the single most important
> continuity artifact — without it, the next call starts from scratch

**0)** **Add:** **what’s** **visible** **on** **landing** **page**

> 1\. Coach logs in → lands on Coach Workspace where they can see at
> minimum: tabs “Today / Tomorrow / This Week,” call rows with status,
> and overview sections (even if they don’t click into them).

**1)** **Confirmations**

> 2\. Coach checks “Confirmation: Received / Pending” (comes from
> WhatsApp confirmation responses tracked back in DTSP).
>
> 3\. Confirmations need not be read-only.
>
> 4\. If “Pending,” coach nudges and records the state (confirmation /
> no-show / new date).

**2)** **Enter** **call** **workspace**

> 5\. Coach clicks tab “Today / Tomorrow / This Week.”
>
> 6\. Coach clicks the relevant call row (teacher name + time). 7. Coach
> reviews (no typing):
>
> ● Click “Pre-session brief.”
>
> ● Click “Agenda / checklist” (always visible/floating while in call
> workspace).

**3)** **Start** **the** **call** **in-platform** **Meet**

> 8\. Coach clicks “Start call (Google Meet).”
>
> 9\. System auto-starts the session record and starts transcript
> capture where supported.
>
> 10.During the call, coach does two required input: ● Click “Focus tag”
> → select one.
>
> ● Fills “Teacher practice markers”

**4)** **No-show** **handling**

> 11\. If teacher does not join, system shows countdown time-to-slot.
>
> 12\. When threshold is reached, system auto-marks outcome “No-show” or
> one-click confirm under completion statuses.
>
> 13\. System prompts reschedule: ● Click “Reschedule.”
>
> ● Click “New time window.” ● Click “Reason category.” ● Click “Save.”
>
> 14\. System increments reschedule counter and auto-escalates on repeat
> (creates escalation item for managers).
>
> 15\. End flow: coach returns to Today list (no summary required for
> no-show, unless you choose to enforce one).

**5)** **Reschedule** **handling**

> 16\. If teacher requests reschedule before or during call, coach
> clicks “Reschedule.” 17. Click “New time window” → choose slot.
>
> 18\. Click “Reason category” → select.
>
> 19\. Click “Save” (counter updates; escalation auto-triggers on
> repeat).

**6)** **Tech-issue** **handling**

> 20.If call fails, coach clicks “Tech issue” outcome/flag.
>
> 21\. Click “Reschedule” → “New time window” → “Reason category = Tech
> issue” → “Save.”

**7)** **Completed** **call** **+** **post-call** **capture**

> 22.Coach clicks “End call.”
>
> 23.System auto-marks completion “Completed” (coach is not asked to
> choose completion).
>
> 24.The “After call.” screen appears with the “AI transcript summary
> (draft).” 25.Coach reviews/edits only these 3 structured fields that
> are part of the summary: ● “What was discussed.”
>
> ● “What was decided.” ● “Next call date.”
>
> 27.Coach confirms “Teacher practice markers” (post-call confirmation).
> Adds qualitative comments if needed
>
> 28.Any fail should be auto-logged and info like duration + some
> summary should be captured.
>
> 29.Coach can retry/move to WhatsApp/phone call etc. (flow should not
> be restrictive).
>
> 30.If they move away and come back, it should show up in “Due action.”

**9)** **Whatsapp** **Summary/collateral/send**

Coach will send the summary an collaterals on WhatsApp from here
(manual).

**10)** **Close** **session**

Replace the close-language with: click “Save” → move to home screen.
